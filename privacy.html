Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 1, 2026

Amhar Works ("we," "us," or "our") operates mobile games including Dream Hatch (the "Games"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard information when you use our Games and visit our website at amharworks.com.

By using our Games or website, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree with this policy, please do not use our Games or website.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information:

Anonymous Analytics Data

We use analytics services to understand how players interact with our Games. This data is collected anonymously and may include information such as gameplay sessions, feature usage, device type, operating system version, language settings, and general geographic region. This information helps us improve game performance, fix bugs, and enhance the player experience. Analytics data does not directly identify you as an individual.

Advertising Identifiers

Our Games may display advertisements provided by third-party advertising partners, including Google AdMob. These partners may collect and use advertising identifiers such as the Google Advertising ID (GAID) on Android devices to deliver personalized or non-personalized advertisements, measure ad performance, and prevent fraud. You can reset or limit the use of your advertising identifier through your device settings.

Crash Reports and Diagnostic Data

We collect crash reports and diagnostic information when our Games encounter errors or technical issues. This may include device model, operating system version, app version, error logs, and stack traces. Crash reports help us identify and fix bugs to improve stability and performance. This data is generally not linked to your personal identity.

Game Progress Data

We may store game progress, achievements, and in-game preferences locally on your device and, where applicable, on our servers to enable save functionality and sync across devices.

2. How We Use Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide, maintain, and improve our Games
  • Analyze usage patterns and optimize gameplay experience
  • Display advertisements through Google AdMob and other advertising partners
  • Diagnose and fix technical issues and crashes
  • Respond to support requests and communicate with users
  • Comply with legal obligations

3. Google AdMob

Our Games use Google AdMob to serve advertisements. AdMob may collect and use data including advertising identifiers, device information, and interaction data to provide and measure ads. For more information about how Google uses data, please visit Google's Privacy Policy and How Google Uses Information from Sites or Apps that Use Our Services.

You can opt out of personalized advertising by adjusting your device settings. On Android, go to Settings > Google > Ads and enable "Opt out of Ads Personalization."

4. Third-Party Services

Our Games may integrate third-party services for analytics, advertising, and crash reporting. These third parties have their own privacy policies governing the collection and use of your information. We encourage you to review the privacy policies of these third-party providers.

5. Data Sharing and Sale

We do not sell your personal data. We may share anonymized or aggregated data with service providers who assist us in operating our Games, such as analytics providers and advertising networks. These providers are contractually obligated to use your information only for the purposes we specify and in accordance with applicable privacy laws.

We may also disclose information if required by law, regulation, legal process, or governmental request, or to protect the rights, property, or safety of Amhar Works, our users, or others.

6. Children's Privacy

Our Games are designed to be enjoyed by players of all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13 (or the applicable age in your jurisdiction) without verifiable parental consent. If you believe we have collected information from a child without proper consent, please contact us at support@amharworks.com and we will take steps to delete such information.

7. Data Security

We implement reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect the information we collect. However, no method of transmission over the Internet or electronic storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

8. Data Retention

We retain collected information only for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this Privacy Policy, unless a longer retention period is required or permitted by law.

9. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have certain rights regarding your personal information, including the right to access, correct, delete, or restrict the processing of your data. To exercise these rights, please contact us at support@amharworks.com.

10. International Data Transfers

Your information may be transferred to and processed in countries other than your country of residence. These countries may have different data protection laws. By using our Games, you consent to the transfer of your information to these countries.

11. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any material changes by posting the updated policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date. Your continued use of our Games after any changes constitutes acceptance of the updated Privacy Policy.
